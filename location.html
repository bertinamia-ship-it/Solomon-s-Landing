How to Get Here

From Los Cabos International Airport (SJD)

Take Highway 1 south towards Cabo San Lucas (approximately 30-40 minutes). Once in Cabo San Lucas, follow signs to the Marina. We're located on the main Marina boardwalk, easily accessible from the marina parking area.

From Downtown Cabo San Lucas

Head towards the Marina district via Blvd. Paseo de la Marina. We're located directly on the Marina boardwalk with beautiful ocean views. Look for our distinctive signage.

Taxi & Uber

Both taxi and Uber services are readily available throughout Los Cabos. Simply tell your driver "Solomon's Landing at the Marina" and they'll know exactly where to take you.
